One unique characteristic of every personality type

Myers Briggs defined all the personality types in terms of various characteristics.

You are either extroverted(E) or introverted (I), sending(S) or intuitive (N), thinking (T) or feeling(F) and perceptive(P) or judging (J).

Although a person can experience all the eight forms at different points in time, only one of each will be obvious most of the time.
